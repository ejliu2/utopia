Utopia Game Rules

Win Conditions

There are no win conditions! Play for 50 turns!

Achieve the high score! The score is determined cumulatively every turn based on current population * current happiness

Buildings and Ships

Construction (🏗️): something is currently building in this spot. Nothing else can be built here

Farms (🌽): generate income when they receive rain (🌧️)! Currently generates 50 gold when it rains

Houses (🏠): boosts population cap by 1, your population cannot exceed the number of houses

Stadiums (🏟️): increases happiness by 1

Fishing Boats (⛵): fishes fish (🐟) in the sea! Icon changes to fishing (🎣) when you land on a fish. Food is increase by 2 if the boat is fishing at the END of the turn

Gun Boats (🚤): destroys pirate ships (🏴‍☠️) when they land on them!

Harbours (⚓): only one in the game (right now). Only place that builds boats (one at a time)

Enemies and Natural Disasters

Earthquakes can happen. Buildings have a chance to be destroyed (💥). You can rebuild on a destroyed building immediately.

Pirate Ships (🏴‍☠️) destroy fishing boats (⛵)! They can be destroyed by gun boats (🚤)

Other

Pirate ships may not move at the moment

Numbers may not be balanced at the moment

Hints

Make sure to build farms (🌽)! They are the only way to make money to build more stuff.
